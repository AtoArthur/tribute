Albert Einstein

The German theoretical Physicist

Here's a timeline of Dr. Albert Einstein's life:

  • 1879 - Born in Ulm, Kingdom of Wurttenberg, German Empire to Herman Einstein and Pauline Koch.
  • 1890 - Moves with his family to Munich, where his father and uncle set up Elektrotechnische Fabrik J. Einstein & Cie, a company that manufactured electrical equipment based on direct current.
  • 1894 - Father and Uncle loose a bid for their company due to financial problems which causes the family to move to Milan.
  • 1990 - Attends Federal polytechnic school in Zurich (Federal teaching diploma).
  • 1903 - Marries his wife Mileva Maric.
  • 1905(Eintein's amazing year) - Publishes 4 groundbreaking papers on;
    1. Photoelectric Effect
    2. Brownian Motion
    3. Special Relativity
    4. Equivalence of Mass and Energy
  • 1905 - Earns a PhD at the University of Zurich.
  • 1911 - Becomes a professor at the German Charles-Ferdinand University in Prague after accepting Austrian citizenship.
  • 1922 - Receives 1921 Nobel Prize in Physics for his services to Theoretical Physics.
  • 1942 - Becomes a member of the National Academy of Sciences.
  • 1955 - Dies at the age of 76.
